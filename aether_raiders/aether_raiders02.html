Aether Empires

MoO + Alpha Centauri with a sprinkle of StarCraft — zoom, pan, colony management, and an optional turn mode.
Wheel = zoom • Right-drag = pan • Space pause • T toggle turns • E End Turn • R Research • P Policies • 0 reset camera

Quick Guide

1) Click a star to select. If you own it, click Manage (bottom bar) to open the colony screen.
2) Build ships in the colony panel. Use Select ship to move then click a glowing reachable star.
3) Open Research (R) and Policies (P) to pick techs and global civics (✓ shows selected).
4) Toggle Turn Mode (T) — then press End Turn (E) to advance.
